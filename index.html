Hello, I'm Steliana Guta, web designer from Amsterdam Area (NL) and this is my homepage.

Most of my work resides on the client side, for which I use hand coded HTML, CSS, Javascript and related graphic tools like Photoshop. Besides this I have some experience with PHP & mySQL and I have done a good deal of work designing templates for various CMS'es: Joomla, Wordpress, Usahidi, Jekyll, etc.

Recently I have taken a good deal of time to learn how to build sites with responsive design as I believe that a user friendly and timeless design has to look equally good on a PC, tablet or smartphone.

RECENT WORKS
powercuts.in

PowerCuts.IN is an initiative to crowdsource information from Twitter, Mobile Web, SMS, Smartphone Apps about power cuts in India. The central idea of this website, as a public service site, is to collect data from people and return it back to people.

The main task was to convert an already existing design idea into a Ushahidi theme. Ajay Kumar, the founder of Power Cuts.in, also wrote in one blog post about my work on their theme.

Technologies: Ushahidi, PHP, CSS, Photoshop
foldinglist.com

Foldinglist is an web application which aim to be the perfect place for all movie lovers from everywhere. Users can add movies in lists as soon as they want to see/ saw a movie. This is great because it help the users to keep the track of what they have seen.

I created the site template and I was also involved in some parts of backend. I did the whole web page: graphic design, CSS code, SQL and some PHP.

Technologies: CSS, Photoshop, SQL, PHP
trac.buzzcapture.com

Buzzcapture is a company specialized in the monitoring and analyzing of social media.They wanted a very minimalist and clean design for their TRAC page.

I completely redesign the TRAC template in order to create a continuity with their client facing site and to offer a "visually quiet" interface for their staff.

Technologies: CSS, Photoshop, TRAC
grupoenasis.ro

Grupo Enasis company has three areas of activity: construction, rental equipment and forest exploitation.

They required a fresh look, usability and functionality for their first site.

I designed a clean, easy to navigate and understand site that reflects their direct and hands on professional attitude.

Technologies: HTML, CSS, Photoshop
